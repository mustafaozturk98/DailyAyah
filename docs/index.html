Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how "Daily Ayah: Quran for All" ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect and process the following types of information:

User Content and App Data

The App may store information you create or save in the App, including favorites, notes, reading progress, quiz or puzzle progress, streaks, XP, notification preferences, selected language, theme settings, and other in-app preferences.

Location Information

The App may request access to your approximate or precise location to calculate prayer times based on your geographic position. Location access is optional.

If you do not grant location permission, some prayer time features may be limited or may use a default location.

Your location may be cached locally on your device. Coordinates may be sent to a third-party prayer time provider, such as AlAdhan API, only for the purpose of retrieving prayer times.

Device, Usage, and Diagnostics Data

We may collect device and usage information such as device model, operating system version, app version, performance data, crash reports, error logs, and interactions with the App.

This helps us maintain, secure, and improve the App.

Advertising Data

The App may use Google AdMob to display ads. Google and its partners may collect advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, and ad interaction data to provide ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and, where permitted, show personalized ads.

Audio Streaming Data

The App streams Quran recitation audio from EveryAyah.com. When you play audio recitations, your device connects to EveryAyah servers to stream the audio content.

We do not collect, store, or process any data related to your audio listening activity.

Notification Data

The App may store notification settings locally to provide daily verses, reminders, and prayer time notifications.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the App.
  • To calculate prayer times based on your location or selected/default location.
  • To stream Quran recitation audio from EveryAyah.com.
  • To send notifications and reminders you enable.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To detect, prevent, and fix crashes, errors, and technical issues.
  • To display and measure advertisements.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the App, users, and our rights.
