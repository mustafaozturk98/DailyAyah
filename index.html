Last updated: May 7, 2026

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

If you create an account or sign in, we may process your email address, Firebase user ID, and basic profile information provided by Google Sign-In.

User Content and App Data

The App may store favorites, notes, reading progress, streaks, notification preferences, selected language, theme settings, and other in-app preferences.

Location Information

The App may request access to your location to calculate prayer times. Location access is optional.

Advertising Data

The App may use Google AdMob to display ads.

Audio Streaming Data

The App streams Quran recitation audio from EveryAyah.com.
